The Mission

True medical innovation requires a balance of softness and rigor. I transform "waste"—such as agricultural surplus—into critical physiological interfaces. By fusing organic materials with low-power embedded architectures (like the Nordic nRF52840), I create wearable systems that are untethered, sustainable, and clinically precise. My work spans from flexible electrode fabrication to ISO 13485 regulatory compliance.
